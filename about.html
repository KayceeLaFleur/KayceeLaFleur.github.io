About Me

This is the page where you can learn about me and my journey to the world of coding. I've always been curious and wondered how things work. In fact as a kid I loved watching Reading Rainbow just to go on tours of place and see how it's made. I also enjoyed Oregon Trail days back in middle school, but my first love was art so I didn't do much tinkering with technology outside of using it. It wasn't until I discovered front-end development which perfectly marries technology with design. I love the rush of seeing what I code appear on screen and I'm ever more enthralled when the code behaves the way I want. When I'm not learning to code, I'm working on UX projects and taking care of my very active and curious toddler.
